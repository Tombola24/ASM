Asset management Page

  • To modify an existing asset, enter the asset ID to locate it. A form with the current details will be displayed for you to update. After saving, a confirmation message will confirm the successful update.

  • Click the button to remove an asset by entering its ID to search and locate it. Confirm the deletion after viewing the asset details, and it will be permanently removed. A confirmation message will appear once the deletion is successful.
