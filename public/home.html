Welcome to the Asset Management System.

  • Use this section to input new assets into the system, including their details such as ID, name, category, purchase date, and more.

  • Use this section to update existing asset details or remove assets from the system. This feature allows you to modify asset information such as ID, name, category, and location, or to delete assets that are no longer needed.

  • The reports section offers various reports and analytics on your assets, helping you make informed decisions based on asset performance and utilization.

  • The upload section allows users to easily add files by selecting them from their device. Once a file is chosen, it will be automatically processed and integrated into the system. This feature supports various file formats for seamless data handling.
